Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

We built Renkli Diller to help you learn languages. We collect only what is necessary to run the app and never sell your data to anyone.

1. Who We Are

Renkli Diller is a mobile language learning application. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at renklidiller@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use the app, we collect:

  • Email address — for account creation and authentication
  • Display name and profile photo — shown on your profile and the leaderboard (if you choose to participate)
  • Learning progress — which sentences you have studied or learned, quiz results, streak count
  • Custom sentences — sentences you add to the app yourself
  • App settings — language preferences, theme, daily goal, notification preferences

We do not collect location data, contacts, camera access, or any data unrelated to language learning.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app's core features
  • To sync your progress across devices
  • To display your rank on the weekly leaderboard (only if you have not opted out)
  • To send daily practice reminders (only if you enable notifications)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to third parties.

4. Leaderboard

By default, your weekly learning activity (as a number only — not your specific words or sentences) and display name are visible to other users on the leaderboard. You can opt out at any time in Settings → Ranking, after which you will appear as anonymous.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We apply row-level security so that users can only access their own data.

6. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive or store your payment card information. Purchase records are managed by the respective platform.

7. Push Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, we schedule local push notifications on your device. No notification content is sent to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings.

8. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Access or update your data within the app
  • Delete your account via Settings → Account → Delete Account, which permanently removes all your data from our servers
  • Opt out of the leaderboard via Settings → Ranking
  • Contact us to request a copy of your data or ask any questions

9. Children's Privacy

Renkli Diller is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests: renklidiller@gmail.com
